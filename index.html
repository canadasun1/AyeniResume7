Embracing the Future of Cybersecurity

In the fast-evolving digital landscape, my journey has been driven by a passion for innovation and a steadfast commitment to safeguarding digital integrity. Educational Foundation

My academic path began with a diploma in Computer Engineering from Technical College, Edo State, Nigeria, followed by a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Benin. Currently, I am expanding my expertise through a PGD in Cybersecurity at Bow Valley College, Calgary, reflecting my dedication to staying ahead in the cybersecurity frontier.

As a Cloud Services Manager, I led my team to new heights of efficiency and security, encapsulating my role as a visionary leader in IT.
